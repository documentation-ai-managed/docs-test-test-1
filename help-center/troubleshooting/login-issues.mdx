---

## Symptoms

- Stuck on login page
- Error: "Invalid credentials"

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````
    Click "Forgot password" and check your email for reset instructions.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    Sometimes cached data blocks login. Try clearing cache and logging in again.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try another browser">
    ````
    If the issue persists, switch browsers or use incognito mode.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
