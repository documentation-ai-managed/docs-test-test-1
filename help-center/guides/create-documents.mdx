---
title: Creating a Documentation Page
description: Quick steps for adding a new doc page in Test test.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    ````
    Select your project from the Test test dashboard.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Page'">
    ````
    Use the sidebar to find and click "Add Page".
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name and save your page">
    ````
    Enter a page name, write content, and hit Save. Your docs are instantly available!
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
