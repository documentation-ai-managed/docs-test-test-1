## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A GitHub account for version control integration

<Callout kind="info">

Test test works best with JavaScript enabled. Disable any strict ad blockers that might interfere with the dashboard.

</Callout>

## Sign Up and Log In

Follow these steps to create your account and access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register Account" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up**, enter your email, create a strong password, and verify your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your credentials, and click **Log In**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

A documentation space organizes your project docs. Create one in seconds.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="From Dashboard" icon="home">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
        Click **New Space** on the dashboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure" icon="settings">
        Enter a name like `my-project-docs`, select public or private visibility, and click **Create**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import Existing" icon="upload">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Import Repo" icon="git-branch">
        Click **Import from GitHub**, authorize Test test, and select your repo.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sync" icon="refresh-cw">
        Choose the branch (e.g., `main`) and click **Import Space**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your First Document

Add a new page to your space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click **New Page** and name it `introduction.mdx`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Frontmatter" icon="code">
    Insert the YAML frontmatter at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Content" icon="pen-tool">
    Add headings and components, then publish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup tabs="MDX Example">
  ```mdx
---
title: My First Page
description: Welcome to my docs.
---

## Hello World

This is your first document!
  ```
</CodeGroup>

<Callout kind="tip">

Save frequently. Test test auto-publishes changes to a live URL like `https://docs.example.com/my-project-docs/introduction`.

</Callout>
